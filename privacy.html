In Short: Some information – such as IP address and/or browser and device characteristics – is collected automatically when you visit our Sites or Apps .

In Short: We may collect information regarding your geo-location, mobile device, and Facebook permissions when you use our apps.

We may obtain information about you from other sources, such as public databases, joint marketing partners, social media platforms (such as Facebook), as well as from other third parties.
